What is ExVertigo's

We are a cottage candy business focused on creating fun and tasty sweets.

What is Cottage Candy?

Cottage candy is candy that is made in the home.

How did we start?

We started due to an accident. Back around 2010, I was making hard candy and decided that I could accurately gauge the temperature without a thermometer. It turns out that I am about as good at gauging temperatures as I am at gauging distances. I ended up pulling my candy off quite early. For those familiar with candy making, you know what that can result in; and for those less familiar, I ended up with a viscous candy instead of a hard candy. This mistake gave me an idea for a new candy that would end up taking me 12 years, and lots of encouragement from my wife, to act on.

Now here we are, and that mistake has become our #1 candy, the Lolli-Shot. We continue to make new fun and tasty candies, and love every minute of it.

Remember:

Don't Be Afraid to Try Something New and Different.

I learned from my journey that mistakes can become successes, and that experimentation, while it may not always result in your desired outcome, can lead to great things.
