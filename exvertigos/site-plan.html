This website is intended to show the imaginative and fun world of ExVertigo’s. Visitors to this site should be able to find the different treats and candy offerings available at ExVertigo’s. Home page

Welcome to ExVertigo’s. We are a cottage candy business focused on creating fun and tasty sweets. What is cottage candy? Cottage candy is candy that is made in the home.

How did something as simple as homemade candy lead to a business? By accident honestly. Back around 2010, I was making hard candy and decided that I could accurately gauge the temperature without a thermometer. It turns out that I am about as good at gauging temperatures as I am at gauging distances. I ended up pulling my candy off quite early. For those familiar with candy making, you know what that results in; and for those less familiar, I ended up with a viscous candy instead of a hard candy. This mistake gave me an idea for a new candy that would end up taking me 12 years, and lots of encouragement from my wife, to act on.

Now here we are, and that mistake has become our #1 candy, the Lolli-Shot. We continue to make new fun and tasty candies, and love every minute of it.

Don't Be Afraid to Try Something New and Different! I learned from this journey that mistakes can become successes; and that experimentation, while it may not always result in your desired outcome, can lead to great things.

Sweets and Treats

Lolli-Shot: What is a Lolli-Shot? • A Lolli-Shot is a viscous* candy that is contained in a syringe. And, before you ask, no, it does contain alcohol. This is an all-ages-friendly candy. *Viscous: having a thick, sticky consistency between solid and liquid – Google Dictionary Why distribute it in a syringe? • We could go with the reason that I heard from a little girl: “…so that you are not scared when you go to the doctor for a shot!”. However, we use a syringe simply as a fun and controlled distribution device.

Lolli-Shot Size and Pricing: Large - $4.00 ea (or 2 for $7.00), Medium - $3.00 ea (or 3 for $8.00), Small - $2.00 ea (or 4 for $7.00), Fun Size - $1.00 ea (or Buy 3 Get 1 Free)

Shaffy: What is a Shaffy? • A Shaffy is a candy that is hard when you start eating it and soft by the end. These candies are cut into about 1/4-inch, square-ish, pieces.

Shaffy Price: $4.00 for a 1.5oz bag

Flavors (for both Lolli-Shot and Shaffy): • B-C-R (Banana-Coconut-Rum) • Butterscotched Apple • Butter Mint • Candy Cane • Cream Soda • Orange Goodness • Peach • Piña Colada • Pumpkin Pie • Pumpkin Spice • Sour Butterscotched Apple • Sour Watermelon • Strawberry Anise • Strawberry Cheesecake
