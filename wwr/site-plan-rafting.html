Overview

Purpose

The puprose of this web page is to increase the customer base for Over Yonder. This web page will offer ease of access to information about our company and the adventure packages available. Visitors to this site will find information on booking group rafting adventures, joining one of our Over Yonder "fill 'er up" group adventures (an economic option for individuals, couples, or small groups), or scheduling a nice relaxing day at our Over Yonder Spa. While every trip at Over Yonder is an adventure, visitors to the website should be able to learn about different adventure levels that will allow fun for every level of adventurere; be it our "Get Your Feet Fet" adventure which provides fun and safety for the whole famly, the "Get Your Blood Pumping" adventure for those looking to take on a little more of the wilds, or the "All In' Adventure for those experienced in white water rafting and looking for the best adrenaline trip you can take.

Audience

The audience for this page will be for anyone looking for adventur from ages 12-99. We are gearing to all party sizes: corporate retreats, family reunion, couples looking for fun, or individuals who are going it alone.
